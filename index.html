SUMMARY:

Legal Battle is a video game that aims to help young international students by raising awareness and provide education on legal resources, while highlighting the importance of seeking professional help.


The legal system is intimidating and difficult to navigate, especially for reactive young people who are not only leaving support systems they grew up with, but stepping into new environments in a new chapter in their life.


Results from research led to the concept of 'Legal Battle' to come naturally, as users wanted engaging storytelling and education from a complex industry.

problem

Struggling international students face unfair work and rental exploits.

I identified that international students in the UK frequently face unfair practices like deposit disputes, contract absences, cash payments, extended shifts, and exploitation due to vulnerabilities such as limited legal knowledge, language barriers, cultural reluctance to seek help, preference for informal networks over professional services, and systemic issues like underfunded legal aid, often leaving them overwhelmed and hesitant to pursue resolutions.
